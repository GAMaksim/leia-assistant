> A: A 3D avatar creates an emotional connection. The WOW effect attracts attention and is memorable.  Hologram is the future that's already available. 

### Q: Who are the competitors?

> A: There are no direct competitors in the CIS market. We're the first with Uzbek language localization and adaptation to the local market.

### Q: How much does support cost?

> A: Minimal. Everything is in the cloud, updates are automatic.  The customer only pays the subscription.

### Q: What if AI gives a wrong answer?

> A: LEIA is trained on the client's context. Plus there's a fallback to "I'll pass your question to a specialist."

### Q: How quickly can it be installed?

> A: Basic installation — 1-2 days. With customization — 1-2 weeks.
